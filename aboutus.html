About Uniglobe

Uniglobe Secondary School, located in the heart of Kathmandu, is a premier institution dedicated to providing students with a 21st-century education in Science, Management, and Law. Established by a distinguished group of professors and academicians, Uniglobe aims to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the dynamic global landscape.

Our institution stands as a testament to quality education, combining traditional values with modern teaching methodologies to create an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and personally.

Diverse Academic Programs

Science

Explore the wonders of the natural world through Uniglobe's comprehensive +2 science program, including specialisations in general science, medical science, engineering, and information technology.

Management

Develop a strong foundation in business principles with Uniglobe's +2 management program. Choose from general, elective, and modern management streams to tailor your education to your career goals.

Law

Uniglobe's +2 law program fosters critical thinking and analytical skills, preparing students for success in legal studies and beyond.

Experienced Leadership

Uniglobe benefits from the guidance of a dedicated board of directors and a highly qualified management team. The school's principal, a seasoned education leader, spearheads academic planning and implementation.

Collaborative Learning

We foster a collaborative learning environment where students and faculty work together to achieve individual and institutional goals. This approach encourages open communication and critical thinking.

Supportive Culture

Uniglobe prioritises a safe and supportive learning environment. We cultivate a strong sense of team spirit among students and staff through trust-based relationships.

Modern Facilities

State-of-the-art facilities designed to enhance learning. Well-equipped classrooms, laboratories, and libraries provide students with the resources they need for academic success.

Your Gateway to a Bright Future

Uniglobe Secondary School offers a unique and stimulating learning environment that prepares students for success in higher education and beyond. With our focus on academic excellence, collaborative learning, and modern facilities, Uniglobe is the perfect choice for students seeking a top-tier education.
